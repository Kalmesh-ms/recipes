Kraft Mac n Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 Box of Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
  • Cheese Powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • Seasoning of you choice
  • 3 cup of water
  • 2 table spoons of butter

Steps

  1. Cooking the mac and cheese(just follow whatever it says back of the package)
  2. Add 1 cup of milk
  3. 2 Tablespoons of butter and your choice of seasoning
  4. Add cheese when it starts to cook
  5. Extra cheese and stir to 8 mins on simmer
