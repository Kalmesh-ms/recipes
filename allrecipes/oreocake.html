Oreo Cake

Ingredients

  • Oreo pack(Duh!)
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cocoa powder
  • 1/4 vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Get a regular cup add 4 Oreos
  2. Add 1/4 cup of milk and break them to little crumbles(use fork)
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar, 1 tablespoon of flour, 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of cocoa powder
  4. Mix it together, Add 1/4 vanilla extract, put an oreo in the middle before you cook it
  5. 1 min in microwave , and you have a delicious cake.
