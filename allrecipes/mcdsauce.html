McDonald's Sweet and Sour Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of apricot preserve
  • 1/4 cup of peach preserve as well
  • 2 tablespoons of corn syrup
  • 5 teaspoons of white vinegar
  • 1/2 spoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 spoon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch

Steps

  1. Mix all Ingredients and put in a sauce pan
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of water and let it simmer for 5 mins
  3. Yo got sweet and sour sauce
